# Audience Profile

## Primary Audience

**Who they are:**
- Age range and demographic profile
- Professional/occupational background
- Geographic location and market context

**What they care about:**
- Main pain points and problems they need solved
- Key goals and aspirations
- Decision-making priorities (cost, quality, reliability, etc.)

**Where they search:**
- Keywords they use when researching solutions
- Search platforms and channels (Google, social media, industry forums, etc.)
- Content types they engage with (articles, videos, case studies, reviews, etc.)

## Secondary Audience

- Title/role
- How they relate to the primary audience
- Their information needs

## Engagement Patterns

- Typical buying cycle and research timeline
- What triggers their searches
- Trust factors and credibility markers important to this audience

## Content Preferences

- Preferred tone and communication style
- Technical depth (beginner-friendly, intermediate, expert)
- Preferred content formats (how-to guides, industry trends, ROI analysis, etc.)

---

*Example: A homeowner (35-55, middle to upper-middle income) searching for roof replacement services would prioritize durability, warranty information, and local reviews. They search terms like "best roofing materials," "roof replacement cost," and "roofing contractors near me." They prefer step-by-step guides and customer testimonials.*
